Color Coding Legend:

Grey - No significant weather (codes <40, wind <15 km/h)

Yellow - Risk (codes 40-59, wind 15-24 km/h)

Orange - Significant Risk (codes 60-79, wind 25-39 km/h)

Red - Severe Risk (codes 80+, wind 40+ km/h)
